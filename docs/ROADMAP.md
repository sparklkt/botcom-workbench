# Roadmap

## v0.1

- Desktop app rebrand.
- BotCom OS sidebar entry.
- Eight-module one-person company operating map.
- Local status API.
- Local review proxy.
- Notification bridge.
- Public documentation.

## v0.2

- Installer-friendly first-run setup.
- In-app environment configuration screen.
- Optional sample media-ops workspace generator.
- Better empty-state guidance when no operating adapters are configured.
- Adapter contracts for CRM, delivery, revenue, assets, and metrics.
- Signed and notarized macOS release.

## v0.3

- Pluggable generators for image, video, captions, voiceover, delivery drafts, and outreach drafts.
- Structured prompt packs for AI worker roles.
- Queue-level quality checks and approval policies.
- Experiment tracking, CRM events, revenue metrics, and metrics import.

## v1.0

- End-to-end AI one-person company cockpit:
  - choose positioning and offers;
  - generate candidate content, assets, outreach, and delivery work;
  - queue high-risk work for review;
  - approve / reject / revise;
  - publish, deliver, follow up, or prepare revenue actions;
  - collect metrics and evidence;
  - run growth and operating review;
  - feed the next production cycle.

## Non-goals

- Engagement spam.
- Credential sharing.
- Hidden scraping.
- Cross-account automation without explicit platform authorization.
